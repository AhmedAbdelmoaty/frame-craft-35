Why V3 Replaced Earlier Attempts:
- V1 was rejected because the HR Manager was not hijabi.
- V2 was rejected before project adoption because the outfit used too many visual colors and did not feel cohesive enough.

What Works:
- Hijabi by design.
- Older than the player analyst characters.
- Cohesive semi-formal suit with a restrained charcoal/white palette.
- Different from the female analyst through age, face structure, hijab color, and HR authority posture.
- Medium blazer length is closer to the target: covers the rear/seat without becoming a long coat.
- Full-body anchor includes hands and feet.
- Turnaround gives usable front, 3/4, side, and back references for later cleanup and spritesheet work.

Watch Before Production:
- Keep the hijab, suit palette, glasses, and blazer length consistent during spritesheet generation.
- The turnaround is still a generated reference sheet, not a final animation source.
- Extracted frames will need transparent background cleanup, scale normalization, and browser preview before runtime use.
