## Review

- Distinct from the player analyst: older, broader, moustache, salt-and-pepper hair, warm blazer, stronger business presence.
- Reads as a Sales Manager / commercial stakeholder, not a teacher or CEO.
- Age range feels appropriate for 45-52.
- Anchor has full body, hands, and feet visible.
- Chroma green anchor background is removable.
- Turnaround includes front, 3/4, side, back, and useful head closeups.
- Clothing is clear and animation-friendly enough for a candidate.

- The blazer length and wider body silhouette should be considered during walk-cycle planning.
